About Us

Baneejjo.com is an online shopping point in Bangladesh. Like any shopping center in the country, Baneejjo.com provides its suppliers and customers all the facilities of an authorized shopping mall. Due to the ever-increasing population and poor traffic conditions in our cities, it is difficult to reach a store and grab your daily necessities in time. We understand the value of your time, and also the hardships you face everyday while trying to reach shopping outlets, waiting in queues – please know that “We Are Here For You”. No Traffic, No Queues, No Loss of Time, Hundreds and Thousands of products to choose from – we deliver your needs to your doorstep all over Bangladesh.

Baneejjo.com is a work-in-progress, and we hope to get better over time. This is a platform, where “Buyers Meet Sellers”, in their own convenience. We are firm believers in using technology and education to improve Bangladesh, and we will continue to invest all our effort in pushing the boundaries to the edge and provide you services in the most advanced method.
We highly appreciate new ideas to continuously improve and recognize the provider. If you have any suggestions on how we can improve, we would like to hear from you. Please do email us at suggestion@bannejjo.com.

We hope you will have a great time doing Baneejjo with us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build an active and creative shopping platform, a sense of understanding and compassion between the buyers and sellers. We want to be a part of people’s life by offering high quality products at affordable price anywhere in Bangladesh.    

We will endeavour to grow the business consistently through positive relationship with our customers and obtain their full satisfaction. We will strive to continuously improve by adapting only those business practices which add value to our clients.

Our vision is to be one of the leading online companies and run our operations is such a way that is economically, socially and environmentally sustainable.
“We Aim to Add Value to People’s Life Everyday”
